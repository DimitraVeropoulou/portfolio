About

Geospatial analysis with a practical environmental focus.

My work combines remote sensing, GIS, and data science to translate spatial data into useful insights. I am especially interested in agricultural monitoring, forest inventory, land-cover change, and decision-support workflows.

I work across R, Python, QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, and Google Earth Engine. I enjoy building workflows that are not only technically correct, but also understandable, reproducible, and useful for people who need to make decisions from spatial data.

Recent work includes sugarcane monitoring and harvest prediction at SmartCane, LiDAR-based tree volume estimation for my MSc thesis, and spatial analysis of false positives in WWF-NL Forest Foresight deforestation predictions.

This portfolio brings together selected projects from research, internships, and applied environmental analysis.
